I CONCOLTRI

The I CONCOLTRI is an international event whose purpose is to allow for discussion and interaction among researchers, academics, politicians and students, in the foundations and applications of the Item Response Theory (IRT).

The statistical models of the IRT and their implementations are currently used by universities, goverment institutions and researchers in Colombia and Latin America. However, there is not a sufficient knowledge about the models and techniques that are being used.

SICS Research Group

The SICS Research Group, is a group of the Universidad Nacional de Colombia which is working in research, innovation and diffusion of IRT. The I CONCOLTRI is organized by the SICS Research Group.

IRTpp

An implementation of the IRT paradigm for the scoring of different instruments measuring latent traits (a.k.a Abilities) and estimating item parameters for a variety of models, this package is highly optimized using Rcpp and carefully written R for the rest of the package, it aims to expand IRT applications to those applications that require faster and more robust estimation procedures.
